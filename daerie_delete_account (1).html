Delete Your Account

Daerie — Android app · Package com.gunlukarkadasim

You can permanently delete your Daerie account and all associated data (journal entries, drawings, goals, chat history, and account information) at any time. There are two ways to do this depending on whether you still have the app installed.

Deleting individual data (without deleting your account)

You don't have to delete your whole account to remove specific data. Within the app, you can permanently delete individual items at any time:

  • An entire notebook: from the main menu, long-press the notebook, then select Delete from the menu that appears (alongside Rename, Change cover, Export as PDF)
  • A single journal entry: open the entry, tap the menu (⋮) in the top-right corner, then select Delete (alongside Pin, Edit, Export as PDF) — or, from the notebook's table of contents, long-press the entry and select Delete
  • Goals: delete a goal from the Goals screen

AI chat history is deleted along with your account if you delete your account entirely (Option 1 below). There is currently no separate option to clear chat history without deleting the account.

These deletions are immediate and permanent — deleted items are not recoverable.

Option 1 — Delete your entire account from within the app (recommended)

Steps

  1. Open Daerie and sign in to your account
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Your account and data are deleted immediately from our active systems once you confirm.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and sign-in information
  • All journal entries, drawings, and covers/themes you selected
  • Goals and AI chat history
  • Subscription linkage to your account (your Google Play subscription itself is managed separately in the Play Store app)
Some information may be retained briefly in backups before automatic rotation, or where we are legally required to keep certain billing records. See our Privacy Policy for details.
