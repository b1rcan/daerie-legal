1Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Daerie ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our Android journaling app. Daerie is developed and operated by an independent developer, contactable at sanatcan1can@gmail.com. By using Daerie, you agree to the collection and use of information as described here. This policy should be read alongside our Terms of Service.

2Information We Collect

Account information

When you sign in with Google Sign-In, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google to create and identify your account.

Journal content

Diary entries, drawings, chosen covers and themes, and any text or images you create inside the app are stored in our database (Firebase Firestore) so they sync across your sessions and devices.

AI chat messages

If you use AI Chat, the messages you send and the AI's replies are processed as described in Section 5. Chat history may be stored to preserve conversation context.

Subscription & purchase data

When you subscribe to Mini or Pro, Google Play Billing shares a purchase token and product ID with us so we can verify and activate your entitlement. We do not receive or store your payment card details — these are handled entirely by Google Play.

Device & diagnostic data

We use Firebase Crashlytics to receive crash reports, which may include device model, OS version, app version, and the state of the app at the time of a crash, to help us fix bugs. Our Firebase project may also use Firebase Analytics to understand general app usage (such as which screens are opened or which features are used) in aggregate. This data is not directly tied to your journal content.

We are currently reviewing exactly which Firebase products are active on our project and will update this section and our Play Console Data Safety form to reflect the final configuration before or shortly after launch.

Local device security

If you enable a PIN or biometric lock, this is handled entirely by your device's operating system and is never transmitted to us or stored on our servers.

4How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and sync the core journaling functionality.
  • To authenticate you and protect your account.
  • To generate AI chat responses and monthly AI reports (Pro).
  • To verify and manage your subscription entitlement.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability.
  • To understand aggregate feature usage and improve the app over time.
  • To communicate with you about your account or respond to support requests.

We do not use your journal content to train AI models, and we do not sell your personal information to advertisers or data brokers.

5AI Features & Third-Party Sharing

Daerie's AI Chat and AI monthly report features are powered by a third-party large language model, accessed via OpenRouter. When you send a message in AI Chat:

  • The text of your message is transmitted to our AI provider to generate a response.
  • This only happens when you actively use AI Chat — it is not applied to entries you keep private in your journal.
  • The provider processes this data to return a response to you; we do not authorize it to use your content to train its own models beyond what is necessary to deliver the response.
  • Free accounts cannot access AI Chat. Mini accounts have a monthly message limit. Pro accounts have effectively unlimited access, subject to fair-use limits.
Your consent: by tapping to send a message in AI Chat, you are actively choosing to share that message with our AI provider for the purpose of generating a reply. You can use every other part of Daerie — writing entries, drawing covers, choosing themes — without ever triggering this data sharing.

6Third-Party Services

Daerie relies on the following third-party service providers to operate:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Functions, Crashlytics) — infrastructure, data storage, crash reporting.
  • Google Play Billing — subscription purchases and payment processing.
  • Google Sign-In — authentication.

Each of these providers processes data under their own privacy terms in addition to this policy. We select providers that maintain industry-standard security and privacy practices.

7Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Firebase Firestore, protected by strict security rules that ensure only you (and, where applicable, our verification systems) can access your entitlement and content records. Firebase encrypts data in transit and at rest at the infrastructure level.

Why we don't use end-to-end encryption

We deliberately do not use end-to-end (client-side) encryption for journal content. With true end-to-end encryption, a forgotten password or lost key means permanent, unrecoverable loss of your journal — we consider this an unacceptable risk for a personal diary app. Instead, we protect your data through Firestore access rules, Firebase's infrastructure-level encryption, and an optional on-device PIN/biometric lock that keeps the app itself inaccessible without your device credentials.

8Data Retention

We retain your account and journal data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account (see Section 9), we permanently delete your journal content, chat history, and account information from our active systems, except where retention is required for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention purposes, or persists briefly in backups before rotation.

9Your Rights & Choices

  • Delete your account: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from Settings inside the app.
  • Access & export: You can contact us to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct your data: Update your account details anytime through Google Sign-In or by contacting us.
  • Opt out of AI features: Simply don't use AI Chat or AI reports — no data is sent to our AI provider unless you actively use these features.
  • Withdraw consent: Where our processing relies on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time by discontinuing use of the relevant feature or deleting your account.
